## 💊 Treatment Breakthroughs

### Prasinezumab — Anti-Alpha-Synuclein Antibody Advances to Phase III
- **Genentech/Roche** announced (June 15, 2025) advancement of prasinezumab into **Phase III** development for early-stage Parkinson's disease.
- Results from **Phase IIb PADOVA trial** and longer-term follow-up data suggest clinical benefit on top of symptomatic treatment.
- Prasinezumab is a **first-in-class anti-alpha-synuclein antibody** targeting a known biological driver of Parkinson's.
- Binds to toxic clumps of alpha-synuclein, preventing them from spreading throughout the brain. Given as monthly IV infusion.
- **Michael J. Fox Foundation** coverage: drugs targeting alpha-synuclein are a major focus — many therapies currently in clinical testing.
- [Genentech Press Release](https://www.gene.com/media/press-releases/15067/2025-06-15/genentech-to-advance-prasinezumab-into-p) | [Roche Press Release](https://www.roche.com/media/releases/med-cor-2025-06-16) | [MJFF Article](https://www.michaeljfox.org/news/drug-potential-slow-or-stop-parkinsons-moves-next-phase-clinical-testing)

## 🏃 Exercise Therapy Research

### Key Finding: Exercise Restores Neural Connections in Parkinson's Patients
- **University Hospitals & Cleveland VA** research (published June 2025, *Clinical Neurophysiology*) found that long-term dynamic cycling exercise may **restore neural connections** in Parkinson's patients.
- Unlike previous studies, this investigation decoded brain changes linked to motor symptom relief — both with second-generation DBS devices and long-term exercise.
- **VA Research news brief**: extended exercise demonstrated it can be an important therapy for Parkinson's disease. [Source](https://www.research.va.gov/news_briefs/news_brief.cfm?RecordID=258232) | [Source](https://news.uhhospitals.org/news-releases/articles/2025/07/researchers-at-uh-and-va-say-exercise-may-restore-neural-connections-in-parkinsons-patients)

### Systematic Review: Optimal Exercise for Cognitive Function in PD
- **PubMed (June 2025)** — Systematic review of 33 RCTs with 1,256 PD patients aimed to identify the most effective exercise modalities and optimal dose ranges for improving cognitive function in Parkinson's disease. [Source](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40848505/)

### Frontiers Review: Exercise as Multitarget Therapy
- Review highlights need to elucidate molecular mechanisms of exercise-induced neuroprotection and establish effective individualized exercise programs for Parkinson's management. [Source](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/aging-neuroscience/articles/10.3389/fnagi.2025.1580029/full)

### Aquatic Aerobic Exercise
- Scoping review on aquatic aerobic exercise prescription in Parkinson's disease — shown potential effects in stabilizing symptoms and disease progression. [Source](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1360859224005904)

## 🧬 Alpha-Synuclein Research

### Prasinezumab (Anti-α-Syn Antibody)
- **Roche/Genentech deal (December 2013)** — licensed monoclonal antibodies targeting aggregated alpha-synuclein.
- **Phase III planned** for early-stage Parkinson's based on PADOVA trial signals.
- Prasinezumab is the lead candidate; many other alpha-synuclein-targeting therapies in clinical testing.

### Brain-Shuttled Antibody (Nature, August 2025)
- New research published in *Nature* (August 2025): a **brain-shuttled antibody targeting alpha-synuclein aggregates** for treatment of synucleinopathies. Authors: Sungwon An, John J. McInnis, et al. [Source](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41531-025-01117-6)
